Greek Chicken

Description

Classic Greek flavors with this easy but yummy Greek Chicken Marinade. It's made with lemon, garlic, and fresh herbs. It takes minutes and calls for some fresh and healthy ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Lemon juice AND zest
  • Garlic
  • Fresh herbs
  • Salt
  • Fresh cracked black peppers

Steps

  1. Prepare the marinade for 30 minutes.
  2. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees and line a large baking sheet with parchment paper or aluminum foil.
  3. Arrange the hicken on the prepared baking sheet, discard any remaining marinade.
  4. Bake until internal temperature reaches 160-165 (165 to be safe) F as read with a digital meat thermomter.
  5. Remove from the oven and allow to rest for 5 minutes before serving.
